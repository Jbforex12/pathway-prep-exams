Pathway Prep - Test Exam Questions (Healthcare Assistant)
Use this file to make a PDF in Word/Google Docs if needed.

1. What is the primary role of a healthcare assistant?
A) Diagnose patients independently
B) Support patient care under supervision
C) Prescribe medication
D) Perform surgery
Answer: B

2. Which action best supports infection control in a care setting?
A) Reuse gloves between patients
B) Skip hand hygiene when busy
C) Follow hand hygiene and PPE protocols
D) Store used PPE in communal areas
Answer: C

3. A patient refuses help with personal care. What should you do first?
A) Force assistance for their safety
B) Ignore the refusal and continue
C) Respect their choice and report concerns to a senior
D) Leave the patient alone for the rest of the shift
Answer: C

4. Which document is essential for recording care given to a patient?
A) Shopping list
B) Care plan or care notes
C) Staff rota only
D) Social media post
Answer: B

5. What does person-centred care mean?
A) Doing tasks as quickly as possible
B) Treating all patients exactly the same
C) Putting the patient's preferences and dignity at the centre
D) Making decisions without consulting the patient
Answer: C

6. You notice a colleague being rough with a resident. What is your duty?
A) Say nothing to avoid conflict
B) Report it through the proper safeguarding procedure
C) Confront them publicly on the ward
D) Post about it online
Answer: B

7. Which vital sign is measured with a sphygmomanometer?
A) Temperature
B) Blood pressure
C) Respiratory rate
D) Oxygen saturation
Answer: B

8. When moving a patient, what should you always consider?
A) Only your own comfort
B) Manual handling and risk assessment
C) Speed above safety
D) Whether anyone is watching
Answer: B

9. A patient with dementia is agitated. A helpful first response is to:
A) Shout to get their attention
B) Restrain them immediately
C) Stay calm, reassure, and remove triggers if safe
D) Leave them in a noisy corridor
Answer: C

10. Confidential patient information should be:
A) Shared with anyone who asks
B) Discussed in public areas
C) Kept secure and shared only on a need-to-know basis
D) Posted on staff group chats
Answer: C
